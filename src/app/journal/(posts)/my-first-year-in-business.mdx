Starting a company can feel like navigating a storm in a small boat. My first year with Coderon was a whirlwind of code, contracts, and coffee.

### 1. Done is Better Than Perfect
In business, momentum is everything. Launch the site, send the proposal, get feedback. Perfection can be the enemy of progress.

### 2. Your Network is Your Net Worth
The most valuable asset I built wasn't code; it was relationships. We don't just build websites, we build partnerships.
